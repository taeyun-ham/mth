Chapter 7: Ready, Set, Go!

"Run, Annie! Run!"
They dashed down the hill, through the grass, past the Pteranodon, and right to the rope ladder.
They scrambled up.
Annie leaped to the window.
The Tyrannosaurus was wandering off.
The monster stopped and turned.
"Duck!" said Jack.
They hunched down.
"Clear coast," said Jack.
"I wish we could go back to Frog Creek," said Jack.
"I left the book and my pack on the hill." "Hurry!" said Annie.
Jack hurried down.
He leaped to the ground.
He raced past the ferns and up the hill.
His pack was lying there.
The valley was filled with Anatosauruses.
Ready! Set! Go!
Jack charged down the hill.
He scooped up his backpack and grabbed the book.
All the dinosaurs were bellowing.
Jack took off.
But the Tyrannosaurus rex was back! And he was standing between Jack and the tree house!
