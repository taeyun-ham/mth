Chapter 6: Dinosaur Valley

"Look at this!" Jack called. "Look what I found!"
She was busy picking a flower from the tree.
Clutching her magnolia flower, she took off down the hill.
Jack grabbed his backpack and raced up the hill.
The valley below was filled with nests.
The nests were filled with tiny dinosaurs!
A gigantic duck-billed dinosaur!
Jack stopped. He didn’t want to get too close.
"Don’t move," he said. "Crawl toward me."
Annie crawled toward Jack.
The dinosaur followed her, still bellowing.
Jack and Annie both bowed their heads and chewed.
The dinosaur grew quiet.
Annie stood up and offered a flower.
The dinosaur moved closer and took the flower.
Annie hurried up the hill. The dinosaur waddled after her.
Jack quickly examined the babies.
He opened the book and read the page.
The monster had two legs, a long tail, and big teeth.
"Tyrannosaurus rex!" Jack whispered.
